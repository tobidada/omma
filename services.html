Services

Facilities Management

Our team of professionals provides Integrated Facilities Management (IFM)service to support client’s business objectives. We delight our customers by providing value-based facilities management service, by not only protecting their investments but also enhancing their value. We achieve this through Planned Preventive Maintenance (PPM) for buildings and services.

We offer a combination of both hard and soft services, depending on clients’ needs. Our services include the following:

  • Electrical
  • Mechanical
  • Power Audit
  • Cooling systems (Installation and maintenance)
  • Space planning
  • Plumbing
  • Interior design
  • Furnishing and fit-outs
  • Fire safety systems
  • Façade cleaning
  • Janitorial
  • Fumigation
  • Landscape
  • Security

Construction

  • Design and build
  • Refurbishment
  • Renovation

Property Management

We help our clients manage their property portfolio, including rentals, sales, and acquisition of landed properties. This includes residential and commercial properties.

  • Property rentals
  • Property sales and acquisition
  • Screening potential customers
  • Marketing properties
  • Relating with Local Authorities
  • Developing a budget for maintenance
  • Managing leases
  • Liaison between tenants and Property owners

Project Management

Our team of highly skilled professionals applies cost management solutions to deliver building projects on behalf of our clients within the agreed timeline and budget while minimizing risks associated with projects.

Our services include:

  • Project monitoring and reporting
